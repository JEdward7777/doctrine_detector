## Question [Calling to Sanctification](../../questions/Calling_to_Sanctification.md)
What command is given to believers regarding their personal sanctification?

## Target answer from notes
Believers in Jesus are commanded to sanctify themselves to God and to be holy in everything they do.

## Concern to grade by
Emphasizing the importance of personal responsibility in the sanctification process.

## Answer given by [gemma Bible Translator](../../answering_models/gemma_Bible_Translator.md)
**Christians are commanded to persevere in personal sanctification through:**

**1. Obedience to God's Word:**

* "Be transformed by the renewing of your mind." (Romans 12:2)
* "Sanctify yourselves, for God is holy." (1 Peter 1:15)

**2. Submission to the Holy Spirit:**

* "But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control." (Galatians 5:22)
* "By the Spirit you are being saved." (Galatians 5:2)

**3. Growth in Christlikeness:**

* "Therefore, as we have opportunity, let us do good to everyone, and especially to those who are of the household of faith." (Galatians 6:10)
* "And be like me in your faith." (1 Corinthians 4:15)

**4. Ongoing Transformation:**

* "For you were once darkness, but now you are light in the Lord. Walk as children of light." (Ephesians 5:8)
* "Continue in the faith, striving for a greater knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ." (2 Peter 3:18)

**5. Daily Pursuit of Holiness:**

* "Holiness should characterize your hearts." (1 Peter 1:15)
* "Examine yourselves to see whether you are in the faith." (2 Corinthians 13:5)
