**The two main sections of the tabernacle were:**

**1. The Outer Court:**

* This area was accessible to anyone, regardless of their status or beliefs.
* It contained the altar of burnt offering, the altar of sacrifice, the laver (washing basin), and the bronze basin.
* These items were used for sacrifices, purification rituals, and washing.


**2. The Holy Place:**

* This area was only accessible to the priests and was where the Most Holy things were kept.
* It contained the golden altar of incense, the table of shewbread, the golden lampstand, and the mercy seat (the throne of God).
* These items symbolized God's presence, provision, and illumination.
