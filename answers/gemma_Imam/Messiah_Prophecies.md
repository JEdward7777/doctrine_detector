## Question [Messiah Prophecies](../../questions/Messiah_Prophecies.md)
What are some of the prophecies about the Messiah mentioned in the Old Testament, and how did Jesus fulfill them?

## Target answer from notes
The prophets wrote about the coming Messiah, including the promise that he would be born from a virgin (Isaiah 7:14) and that he would be a descendant of King David (2 Samuel 7:12-13). Jesus fulfilled these prophecies through his virgin birth and lineage.

## Concern to grade by
Understanding the significance of prophecies fulfilled by Jesus strengthens faith in Him as the Messiah.

## Answer given by [gemma Imam](../../answering_models/gemma_Imam.md)
**Prophecies about the Messiah mentioned in the Old Testament:**

**1. The Suffering Servant:**
- Isaiah 53:3-5 describes a suffering servant who would bear the sins of many.
- Jesus fulfilled this prophecy through his sacrificial death on the cross.

**2. The Branch from David:**
- Jeremiah 23:5 and other prophecies foretold that the Messiah would be a descendant of King David.
- Jesus is genealogically descended from David.

**3. The Prince of Peace:**
- Isaiah 9:6 speaks of a ruler who will bring peace to the land.
- Jesus brought peace to the world through his teachings and example.

**4. The Suffering Son:**
- Psalms 22 and other prophecies predict a Son who would be mocked, ridiculed, and ultimately executed.
- Jesus experienced these sufferings during his crucifixion.

**5. The Great High Priest:**
- Psalm 110:1 describes a Messiah who would be a great high priest.
- Jesus is considered the ultimate high priest, offering himself as a sacrifice for sin.

**6. The Triumphant King:**
- Daniel 7:14 prophesies a coming king who will rule with dominion and receive eternal glory.
- Jesus will ultimately return in triumph as King of Kings.


**How Jesus fulfilled these prophecies:**

* **Suffering Servant:** Jesus' sacrificial death and resurrection fulfilled the prophecy of the Suffering Servant.
* **Branch from David:** Jesus' genealogy from David confirms his fulfillment of this prophecy.
* **Prince of Peace:** Jesus' teachings and example of love and forgiveness brought peace to the world.
* **Suffering Son:** Jesus' suffering and crucifixion fulfill this prophecy.
* **Great High Priest:** Jesus' role as mediator between God and humanity fulfills this prophecy.
* **Triumphant King:** Jesus' future return as King of Kings fulfills this prophecy.

**These prophecies point to Jesus as the fulfillment of the Messianic prophecies in the Old Testament, establishing his identity as the Son of God and the Savior of the world.**
