What is the primary purpose of Jesus using parables in his teachings?

## Target answer from notes
Jesus used parables to explain or teach moral truths, revealing truth to his disciples while hiding it from those who did not believe.

## Concern to grade by

## Answer given by [gemma Imam](../../answering_models/gemma_Imam.md)
**The primary purpose of Jesus using parables in his teachings was to:**

* **Make his teachings accessible and understandable to ordinary people:** Parables used familiar situations and concepts to illustrate spiritual truths that might otherwise be difficult to grasp.
* **Challenge preconceptions and cultural biases:** By presenting familiar things in new contexts, Jesus challenged the listeners' assumptions and revealed new perspectives.
* **Engage the hearts and imaginations of his audience:** Parables were designed to evoke emotions, inspire reflection, and stimulate action.
* **Illustrate spiritual concepts and principles:** Parables used relatable scenarios to teach about concepts such as faith, forgiveness, love, and the nature of God.
* **Open up spiritual understanding to those who were open to it:** Jesus used parables to convey profound spiritual truths without resorting to direct statements or explanations that might be rejected by his listeners.
