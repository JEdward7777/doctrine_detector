**Differences between the two concepts:**

* **Nature of inheritance:** In Islam, inheritance refers to the physical transfer of property or assets. In Christianity, receiving God's promises is primarily understood as a spiritual transformation and relationship with God.
* **Basis for entitlement:** In Islam, inheritance is based on legal regulations and blood relationships. In Christianity, receiving God's promises is based on faith in Jesus Christ and obedience to God's laws.

**Conclusion:**

While there are parallels between the concepts of being an heir in Islam and receiving God's promises in Christianity, they are distinct in nature and basis. The Muslim concept focuses on physical inheritance, while the Christian concept emphasizes spiritual transformation and relationship with God.
