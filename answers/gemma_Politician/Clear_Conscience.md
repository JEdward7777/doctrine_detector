**Having a clear conscience in the context of Christian teachings means:**

* **Integrity:** Living in alignment with Christian values, principles, and ethics. This includes honesty, fairness, and compassion.


* **Alignment with Scripture:** Consulting and adhering to the teachings of the Bible, which is the ultimate source of moral guidance for Christians.


* **Honesty:** Being truthful in words and actions, avoiding deception and manipulation.


* **Accountability:** Taking responsibility for one's actions and seeking forgiveness when necessary.


* **Compassion:** Showing empathy and concern for the well-being of others, regardless of their beliefs or backgrounds.


* **Purity of Heart:** Having a sincere and clean heart free from malice, greed, and other destructive thoughts and intentions.


* **Alignment with Christian Values:** Living in accordance with Christian values such as love, mercy, forgiveness, and peace.
