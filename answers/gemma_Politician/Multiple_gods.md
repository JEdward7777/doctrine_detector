Missionaries should provide clear biblical teaching that explains the doctrine of the Trinity and God's singular nature.

## Concern to grade by

**1. Focus on Beliefs, Not Idolatry:**

* Emphasize that Christians believe in one God, who is the creator of all things.
* Explain that the concept of multiple gods is often a cultural or philosophical belief, not a core belief of Christianity.


**2. Counter Misinformation:**

* Provide accurate information about Christian beliefs and practices to address any misconceptions.
* Use clear and respectful language that avoids sensationalism or judgmentalism.


**3. Contextualize Cultural Influences:**

* Acknowledge that the idea of multiple gods may be prevalent in certain cultures where Christianity is practiced.
* Explain that cultural and linguistic nuances can influence religious beliefs and interpretations.


**4. Emphasize the Uniqueness of Christ:**

* Highlight the uniqueness of Jesus Christ as the Son of God and the only mediator between God and humanity.
* Explain that Christians believe Jesus is the fulfillment of all biblical prophecies.


**5. Use Inclusive Language:**

* Avoid language that suggests Christians are polytheists or endorse any beliefs that contradict their core beliefs.
* Use inclusive language that respects the diversity of beliefs and perspectives within Christianity.
