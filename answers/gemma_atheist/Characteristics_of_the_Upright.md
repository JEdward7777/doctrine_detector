**Qualities Defining an 'Upright' Person in the Biblical Context:**

**1. Ethical Integrity:**

* Adherence to moral principles and values
* Uprightness in speech and actions
* Accountability for wrongdoing
* Willingness to right wrongs

**2. Justice and Fairness:**

* Upholding the rights of others
* Treating people with respect and impartiality
* Seeking justice in social and political systems
* Avoiding bias and corruption

**3. Humility and Selflessness:**

* Recognizing the greatness of God and the needs of others
* Willingness to serve and sacrifice for the common good
* Absence of arrogance and pride

**4. Integrity of Character:**

* Consistency in thoughts, words, and actions
* Adherence to moral principles regardless of circumstances
* Avoidance of deception and hypocrisy

**5. Fear of God:**

* Reverence for the divine
* Recognition of God's sovereignty and justice
* Avoidance of actions that displease God

**6. Obedience to God's Laws:**

* Adherence to religious laws and ethical principles
* Willingness to follow God's commands even when they are challenging or unpopular

**7. Compassion and Empathy:**

* Understanding and caring for the suffering
* Concern for the needs of others
* Willingness to help those in need

**8. Purity of Heart:**

* Freedom from sin and defilement
* Uprightness in thought and action
* Alignment with God's standards of righteousness

**9. Authenticity and Sincerity:**

* Genuine faith and commitment to righteousness
* Uprightness in intentions and actions
* Absence of hypocrisy or pretense

**10. Resilience and Perseverance:**

* Ability to withstand challenges and temptations
* Uprightness in the face of adversity
* Persistence in pursuing righteousness despite obstacles
