## Terms used to refer to the Lord's Supper in Christian communities:

**Formal/Traditional:**

* The Lord's Supper
* Eucharist
* The Bread and Wine
* Holy Communion

**Informal/Popular:**

* Communion
* Break of Bread
* The Supper
* Table Fellowship

**Cultural/Denominational variations:**

* **Catholic:** The Eucharist, Precious Blood
* **Protestant:** Communion, The Lord's Table
* **Orthodox:** The Divine Liturgy, Mysteries of Christ
* **Charismatic/Renewal:** The Lord's Meal, The Feast of Love

**Scholarly/Philosophical:**

* The Sacred Meal
* The Lord's Meal
* The Eucharist as Meal
* The Symbolic Meal

**Inclusive/Neutral:**

* The Agape Meal
* The Community Meal
* The Shared Meal of Love
