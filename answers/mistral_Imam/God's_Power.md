## Answer given by [mistral Imam](../../answering_models/mistral_Imam.md)
 In Islamic belief, the term "Almighty" (Arabic: Al-Qahhar or Al-Khaaliq) signifies the supreme power, omnipotence, and ability of Allah (God). It emphasizes that Allah has absolute control over all aspects of His creation, and nothing happens in the universe without His knowledge and will. He is capable of doing anything and everything, and He is not limited by space or time. This attribute underscores Allah's sovereignty, power, and majesty, as well as His ability to create, sustain, and govern the entire cosmos according to His infinite wisdom and justice.
