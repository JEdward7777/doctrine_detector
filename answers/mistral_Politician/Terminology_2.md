## Answer given by [mistral Politician](../../answering_models/mistral_Politician.md)
 In Christian communities, the Lord's Supper is known by various terms that reflect the denomination or tradition. Here are some of the commonly used names for this sacrament:

1. Eucharist (Catholic, Orthodox, Anglican, and many Protestant churches)
2. Holy Communion (Used in many Protestant denominations such as Lutherans, Methodists, Presbyterians, etc.)
3. The Sacrament of the Altar (Lutheran Church)
4. The Lord's Table (Some Baptist churches and other non-denominational churches)
5. The Breaking of Bread (some Quaker meetings)
6. Agape Feast (Eastern Orthodox and some charismatic churches)
7. Mass (Roman Catholic Church)
8. Divine Liturgy (Orthodox Church)
9. Holy Mystery (Episcopal Church)
10. The Blessed Sacrament (Catholic tradition)
