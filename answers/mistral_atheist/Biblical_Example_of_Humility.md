## Question [Biblical Example of Humility](../../questions/Biblical_Example_of_Humility.md)
Can you provide a biblical example of a character who exemplified humility?

## Target answer from notes

## Answer given by [mistral atheist](../../answering_models/mistral_atheist.md)
 Absolutely! One such biblical figure who exemplified humility is Jesus Christ himself, as portrayed in the New Testament. Despite being the Son of God, he often humbled himself and served others (Philippians 2:5-8). In the Gospels, we see many instances where Jesus demonstrates this humility, such as washing his disciples' feet (John 13:1-17), spending time with tax collectors and sinners (Matthew 9:10-13), and healing the sick without seeking personal gain.

Another example of a biblical character who embodied humility is Moses, the leader of the Israelites in the Old Testament. Despite being called by God to lead millions of people out of slavery in Egypt, Moses was initially hesitant and humble, saying "Who am I that I should go to Pharaoh and bring the Israelites out of Egypt?" (Exodus 3:11). He continued to demonstrate humility throughout his leadership, often attributing God's actions to Him rather than taking credit himself.

In both of these examples, we can see the importance of humility as a virtue in the biblical tradition. Humility enables individuals to recognize their limitations and rely on a higher power, whether that be God or the collective wisdom of others. This humble mindset allows for cooperation, collaboration, and growth within communities.
