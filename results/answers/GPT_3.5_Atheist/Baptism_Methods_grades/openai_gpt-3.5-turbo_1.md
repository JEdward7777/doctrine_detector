1. **Immersion**: This is the most widely practiced form of baptism in many Christian traditions. It involves completely submerging the individual in water, symbolizing burial and resurrection with Christ.

2. **Affusion**: This method involves pouring water over the head of the individual, symbolizing purification and renewal.

3. **Aspersion**: This form of baptism involves sprinkling water on the individual, symbolizing the cleansing power of the Holy Spirit.
