The key components of the Mosaic Covenant included:
1. The giving of the Ten Commandments and other laws as a moral and ethical code for the Israelites to follow.
2. Promises of blessings for obedience and curses for disobedience.
3. Regulations for worship, including the construction of the tabernacle and the establishment of the priesthood.
4. Provisions for sacrifice and atonement for sins through the sacrificial system.
