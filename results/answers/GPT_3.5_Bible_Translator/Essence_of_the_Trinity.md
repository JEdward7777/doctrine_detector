The Father is often seen as the creator and sustainer of the universe, the Son as the one who became incarnate as Jesus Christ to save humanity, and the Holy Spirit as the one who empowers and indwells believers. While each person of the Trinity has unique roles, they are united in purpose and work together in perfect harmony.

It is important to note that the Trinity is a mystery that goes beyond human understanding. It is a doctrine that is revealed in Scripture and has been affirmed by the Christian church throughout history.
