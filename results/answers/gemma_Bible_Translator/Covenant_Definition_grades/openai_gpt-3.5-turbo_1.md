A covenant in the Bible is a legal and relational agreement between two or more parties, established upon specific terms and conditions. It involves:

**1. Intentionality:** A conscious decision to enter into the agreement.
**2. Mutuality:** Involvement of two or more parties.
**3. Explicit terms:** Specific conditions and stipulations.
**4. Exchange:** A reciprocal action or promise.
