**1. Local Assembly:**

* Primarily refers to the gathered community of believers in a specific location.
* Emphasizes the local fellowship and worship. (See Acts 2:42, 1 Corinthians 11:18)

**2. Visible Body of Christ:**

* Represents the entire community of believers scattered throughout the world.
* Emphasizes the mystical union with Christ and the global nature of the Church. (See Ephesians 1:23, Colossians 1:18)

**3. Spiritual Institution:**

* Represents the divinely ordained institution established by Christ for the salvation and growth of believers.
* Emphasizes the role of the Church as a spiritual entity with specific functions and purposes. (See Hebrews 10:25, 1 Peter 5:4)

**4. Kingdom of God:**

* Represents the spiritual realm where God's rule prevails through the Church.
* Emphasizes the cosmic implications of the Church as a force for good in the world. (See Revelation 5:10)

**Therefore, the term 'church' in the New Testament encompasses:**

* **Local communities** of believers
* The **universal body of Christ**
* The **spiritual institution** established by Christ
* The **kingdom of God** that is advancing through the Church.
