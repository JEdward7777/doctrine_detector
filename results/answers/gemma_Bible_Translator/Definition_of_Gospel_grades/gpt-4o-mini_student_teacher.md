## Biblical Understanding:

Within the Bible, the 'gospel' refers to the central message of Christianity: **the good news of salvation through Jesus Christ.** This message includes:

* **His birth** as the Son of God
* **His life** as the perfect sacrifice for sin
* **His death** on the cross as the payment for sin
* **His resurrection** from the dead as the victory over sin and death
* **His ascension** to heaven as the triumphant King

* **Eternal life:** Jesus' resurrection guarantees our own resurrection and entrance into heaven.
