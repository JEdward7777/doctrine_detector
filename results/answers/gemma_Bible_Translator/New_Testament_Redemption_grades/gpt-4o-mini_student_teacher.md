**New Testament:**

* Focus on transformation of heart and life through the Holy Spirit.
* Purity is seen as a product of faith and a reflection of God's love.

* New Testament: To reflect God's love and righteousness, and to live in accordance with God's will.

* Old Testament: Ritualistic purity.
