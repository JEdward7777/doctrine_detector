**Listening to God connects to becoming wise in several ways:**

**1. Reveals God's wisdom:**

* God's Word is a fountain of wisdom and insight (Psalm 119:105).
* Listening attentively to God's spoken word through prayer and scripture unlocks profound understanding and practical wisdom.

**2. Teaches us God's character:**

* God's character is defined by wisdom, justice, and love (1 John 3:18).
* By listening to God, we learn to discern right from wrong, prioritize values, and make decisions aligned with his character.

**3. Illuminates our own ignorance:**

* Humility and openness to God's voice is key to recognizing our own limitations and areas of growth.
* Listening to God reveals areas where we need wisdom and guides us towards better understanding.

**4. Guides our actions:**

* God's wisdom transcends human knowledge and provides guidance for every aspect of life (Proverbs 3:5-6).
* By listening to God, we receive practical insights for navigating challenges, making decisions, and living out our faith.

**5. Fosters spiritual growth:**

* Listening to God fosters a close and intimate relationship with him.
* As we engage in heartfelt conversations with our Heavenly Father, we grow in wisdom and spiritual maturity.

**6. Provides discernment:**

* In a world filled with conflicting voices and information, God's voice offers clarity and discernment (James 1:5).
* Listening to God allows us to discern truth from falsehood and make informed decisions.

**7. Produces fruitfulness:**

* Wisdom applied according to God's principles leads to fruitfulness (James 3:17).
* By listening to God, we receive the wisdom we need to live out our faith and bear spiritual fruit.
