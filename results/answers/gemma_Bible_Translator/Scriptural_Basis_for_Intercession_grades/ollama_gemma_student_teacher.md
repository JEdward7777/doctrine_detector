**Bible Verses on Intercession:**

**1. Romans 8:26-27**
"Likewise the Spirit helps us in our weakness: for we do not know how to pray as we should. But the Spirit Himself intercedes for us with groanings that words cannot express. "I urge that supplications, prayers, intercession, and thanksgiving be made for everyone. For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Savior."

"Continue steadfastly in prayer, being watchful in it with thanksgiving."

"Praying always with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit, and being watchful to this with all perseverance and supplication for all the saints."

**5. Hebrews 7:25**
"Therefore he is able to save completely those who approach God through him, since he always lives to make intercession for them."

**6. James 5:13-18**
"Is anyone among you suffering? Let him pray. Is anyone cheerful? Let him sing praise. Pray for each other, that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous man is powerful in the sight of God."

**7. 1 Peter 3:12**
"For you are not without a spirit, but you are servants of God, who stand by him for good with the saints, who suffer alongside you and rejoice in hope of eternal life."

**8. Luke 18:1-8**
"Jesus taught them that they must pray always and never give up."

**Insights from these Verses:**

* Intercession is an essential aspect of Christian life.
* The Holy Spirit plays a key role in intercession.
* Supplication and thanksgiving are vital components of intercession.
* Prayer should be ongoing and persistent.
* Intercession is effective because it is done in faith and according to God's will.
