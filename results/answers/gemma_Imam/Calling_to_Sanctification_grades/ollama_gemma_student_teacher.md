## [Answer](../Calling_to_Sanctification.md) by [gemma Imam](../../../answering_models/gemma_Imam.md)
**The Quran and Sunnah provide guidance on personal sanctification for believers:**

**1. Quranic Commandments:**

- Surah Al-A'raf, verse 23: "Say, 'I am obedient to God and I am on a way of righteousness.'"
- Surah An-Nisa', verse 83: "And be conscious of God as He should be conscious of Himself."

**2. Sunnah Practices:**

- **Dhikr:** Recitation of remembrance of God.
- **Salah:** Performing five daily prayers.
- **Zakat:** Giving charity to the needy.
- **Sawm:** Fasting during the month of Ramadan.
- **Taqwa:** Maintaining a sense of fear and awe of God in all actions.

**3. Imamly Guidance:**

- Engage in regular dhikr and supplication.
- Prioritize acts of kindness and compassion.
- Engage in Quranic study and contemplation.
- Practice mindfulness and self-reflection.
- Seek guidance from reliable scholars and teachers.

**4. Additional Tips:**

- Consistency and regularity in practices are key.
- Personal growth is an ongoing journey, not a destination.
- Surround yourself with positive influences and avoid harmful ones.
- Engage in acts of charity and volunteerism.
- Prioritize self-improvement in all aspects of life.
