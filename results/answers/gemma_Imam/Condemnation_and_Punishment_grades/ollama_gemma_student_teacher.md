"The Lord is faithful in love and forgiveness; abundant in mercy."

This verse highlights the immense love and forgiveness of Allah. It reminds us that Allah is always willing to forgive us of our sins and mistakes. * **Emphasize the importance of context:** The application of punishment should always be fair and appropriate, considering the specific context and circumstances.
