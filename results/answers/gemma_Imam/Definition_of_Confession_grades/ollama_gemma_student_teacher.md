**Islamic Perspective:**

In Islam, there is a concept of repentance (taubah) which is similar to confession in Christianity. Repentance involves:

- Recognizing the need for forgiveness.
- Expressing sincere regret.
- Making amends for wrongdoing.
- Strengthening one's resolve to avoid future sins.

- Sinfulness is a universal reality.

- **Nature of forgiveness:** In Islam, forgiveness is primarily seen as a gift from God, while in Christianity, it is also believed that forgiveness is achieved through the atonement of Jesus Christ.
