**Almighty** (الرحيم) is a divine attribute that signifies God's boundless power, sovereignty, and omnipotence.

**In Islamic belief:**

* The Almighty is the ultimate source of all power and control.
* His authority extends over all of creation, from the heavens to the earth and from the smallest atom to the largest planet.
* His power is limitless and beyond human comprehension.

**The term 'Almighty' highlights:**

* **God's absolute sovereignty:** God's power is not limited or shared with anyone or anything.
* **God's boundless energy:** God possesses an infinite reservoir of energy and vitality.
* **God's omnipotence:** God can do anything that is possible, without restriction or limitation.
* **God's perfect control:** God exercises complete authority over all events and circumstances.

**The Quran and Islamic tradition frequently mention the Almighty:**

> "He is the Almighty, the Most Merciful." (Quran 6:100)

> "Say, 'He is the Almighty.' He encompasses the heavens and the earth." (Quran 2:255)

**In Islamic teachings, the Almighty is associated with:**

* His immense power and sovereignty
* His boundless love and mercy
* His complete control over destiny
* His ultimate authority over all creation

**The term 'Almighty' reminds Muslims of the immense power and glory of God, and encourages them to rely on His strength and seek His guidance in all aspects of life.**
