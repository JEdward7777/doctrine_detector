**2. The Branch from David:**
- Jeremiah 23:5 and other prophecies foretold that the Messiah would be a descendant of King David.
- Jesus is genealogically descended from David.

**3. The Prince of Peace:**
- Isaiah 9:6 speaks of a ruler who will bring peace to the land.
- Jesus brought peace to the world through his teachings and example.

**4. The Suffering Son:**
- Psalms 22 and other prophecies predict a Son who would be mocked, ridiculed, and ultimately executed.
- Jesus experienced these sufferings during his crucifixion.

**5. The Great High Priest:**
- Psalm 110:1 describes a Messiah who would be a great high priest.
- Jesus is considered the ultimate high priest, offering himself as a sacrifice for sin.

**6. The Triumphant King:**
- Daniel 7:14 prophesies a coming king who will rule with dominion and receive eternal glory.
- Jesus will ultimately return in triumph as King of Kings.

* **Branch from David:** Jesus' genealogy from David confirms his fulfillment of this prophecy.

* **Suffering Son:** Jesus' suffering and crucifixion fulfill this prophecy.
* **Great High Priest:** Jesus' role as mediator between God and humanity fulfills this prophecy.
* **Triumphant King:** Jesus' future return as King of Kings fulfills this prophecy.
