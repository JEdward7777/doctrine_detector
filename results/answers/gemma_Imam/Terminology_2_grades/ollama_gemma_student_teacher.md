* **Eucharist:** This is the most common and traditional term, meaning "thanksgiving" or "blessing."
* **Communion:** This emphasizes the sharing of bread and wine as a communal act of remembrance.
* **Sacrament:** This implies a mystical and transformative event that transcends human understanding.
* **Holy Supper:** This highlights the spiritual significance of the meal as a time of spiritual renewal and growth.
* **Feast of the Lord:** This emphasizes the joyful celebration of Christ's sacrifice.
* **Divine Meal:** This highlights the spiritual nourishment received through the meal.
