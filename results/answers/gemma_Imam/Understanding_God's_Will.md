**Seeking God's will in our everyday decisions** is a central tenet of Islamic faith. It means aligning our choices and actions with the guidance of Allah as revealed in the Quran and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon them).

**Here's how we can seek God's will in our daily lives:**

**1. Consult the Quran and Sunnah:**

* Seek guidance from the Quran, the eternal word of Allah, and the Sunnah, the record of the Prophet's actions and sayings.
* Consult Islamic scholars and teachers for deeper understanding.

**2. Reflection and Dua:**

* Reflect on the potential consequences of our actions.
* Make du'a (prayer) to Allah, asking for guidance and wisdom.
* Seek clarity and peace in prayer.

**3. Consider the Principles of Islamic Law:**

* Apply Islamic principles such as halal (lawful), haram (forbidden), and ma'ruf (recommended).
* Prioritize actions that foster faith, piety, and justice.

**4. Seek Balance and Moderation:**

* Avoid extremes and strive for balance in our decisions.
* Consider the rights of others and the wider community.

**5. Be Aware of Allah in Everything We Do:**

* Maintain awareness of Allah's presence in our actions and intentions.
* Perform actions with sincerity and humility.

**Examples of seeking God's will:**

* Choosing between two job offers, considering which aligns better with Islamic principles and values.
* Making financial decisions, prioritizing spending on necessities and charitable causes.
* Selecting a spouse, seeking compatibility and upholding Islamic guidelines for marriage.

**Remember:**

* Seeking God's will is a continuous journey, not a destination.
* Be patient and persistent in your efforts.
* Allah guides those who seek him.
