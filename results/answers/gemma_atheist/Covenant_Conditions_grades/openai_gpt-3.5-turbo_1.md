## Conditional and Unconditional Covenants in the Bible:

**Unconditional Covenants:**

These are promises God makes to us, regardless of our actions or merit. 

* **Covenant of Grace:** God promises unconditional love and forgiveness to all who believe in Jesus Christ (John 3:16).
* **Covenant of Redemption:** God promises to rescue us from sin and death through Christ's sacrifice (Romans 5:8).
* **Covenant of Faithfulness:** God promises to be faithful to us, even when we are faithless (2 Timothy 4:10).


**Conditional Covenants:**

These are promises God makes to us based on our faithfulness or obedience.

* **Covenant of Works:** God promised Adam paradise if he obeyed God's commands (Genesis 2:16-17).
* **Covenant of Law:** God made a covenant with Moses where obedience to the Ten Commandments would guarantee blessing (Exodus 20:23-25).
* **Covenant of Circumcision:** God promised Abraham that his descendants would be blessed if they circumcised themselves (Genesis 17:9-10).
