**Biblical perspectives on covenant:**

* **Hebrew Bible (Tanakh):**
    * Covenants are seen as divine initiatives, where God initiates the agreement and promises unconditional love and fidelity.
    * Key concepts include blood-stained sacrifices and rituals as symbolizing the covenant.
