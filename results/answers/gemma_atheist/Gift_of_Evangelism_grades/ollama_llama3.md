* Evangelists tend to engage more actively with specific cultures and communities, seeking to convert them to Christianity.

* Evangelists often prioritize evangelistic activities, such as preaching, outreach events, and tract distribution.

* Evangelists may draw authority from their personal experiences or charismatic leadership qualities.
