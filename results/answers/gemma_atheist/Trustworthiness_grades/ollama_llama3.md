**3. Transparency and accountability:**

* Share relevant data and sources for scrutiny.

**4. Compassion and empathy:**

* Treat others with respect, regardless of their religious or cultural background.
* Listen attentively to diverse viewpoints without interrupting.
* Demonstrate sensitivity to the potential harm caused by biased or misleading information.


**5. Humility and open-mindedness:**

* Acknowledge the possibility that your understanding of the world may be limited.
* Remain open to new information and perspectives that challenge your assumptions.
* Avoid arrogance and intellectualism.
