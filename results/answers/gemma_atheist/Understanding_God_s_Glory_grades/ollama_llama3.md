* **Biblical:** Glorification is eternal and centered on God's character, which remains constant regardless of human actions. 
* **Worldly:** Glory is temporary and often fleeting, lasting only as long as the achievement or possession exists.

* **Worldly:** Chasing worldly glory can lead to pride, envy, and dissatisfaction.
