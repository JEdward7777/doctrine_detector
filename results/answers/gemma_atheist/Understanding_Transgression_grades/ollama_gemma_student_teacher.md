* **Legal transgression:** Violation of God's laws or commands, such as adultery or Sabbath breaking.
* **Covenant transgression:** Breaking the covenant relationship with God through actions or attitudes that demonstrate disobedience or disrespect.

**The consequences of transgression are severe:**

* Sin separates people from God and brings judgment.
* Violation of God's law can result in punishment and spiritual harm.
* Transgression undermines the authority and sovereignty of God.

**The idea of transgression is central to many biblical themes:**

* **Original sin:** The first transgression was Adam and Eve's eating of the forbidden fruit in the Garden of Eden.
* **Fall of man:** Humanity's transgression led to spiritual and physical death.
* **Redemption:** Christ's sacrifice atoned for the sins of transgression.
* **Eternal punishment:** Transgression has eternal consequences for those who reject God's forgiveness.
