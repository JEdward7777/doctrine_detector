Similarly, a priest may be consecrated to perform specific religious duties and minister to others. In the case of objects, items like altars, chalices, or vestments may be consecrated for use in the sacraments and liturgical services.

It also reflects a broader theological understanding of holiness and the belief that certain things can be made “holy” through this act of dedication.
