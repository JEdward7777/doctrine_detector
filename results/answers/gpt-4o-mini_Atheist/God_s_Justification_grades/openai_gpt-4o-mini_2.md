1. **Concept of Justification**: Justification is often understood as a legal declaration by God. 2. **Faith and Grace**: Justification is not based on human works or merit but is a gift of grace. This means that it is unearned and cannot be achieved through good deeds; rather, it is received through faith. The idea is that faith allows individuals to accept the grace offered by God.

4. **Transformation**: While justification is a legal status conferred upon believers, it is often accompanied by the process of sanctification, which entails personal transformation and moral growth.
