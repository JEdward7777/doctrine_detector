1. **Faith in Jesus Christ**: A central tenet is belief in Jesus as the Son of God and the Savior of humanity. 4. **Involvement in the Community of Believers**: Christians are typically part of a community known as the Church, which is called to fellowship, worship, and support one another. Hebrews 10:24-25 encourages believers to meet together and spur one another on toward love and good deeds.

5. **Transformation of Life**: Being a Christian also implies a transformed life marked by the fruit of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22-23), which includes love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

In summary, a Christian is someone who believes in Christ, repents of sin, follows His teachings, participates in the church community, and experiences a transformative relationship with God through the Holy Spirit.
