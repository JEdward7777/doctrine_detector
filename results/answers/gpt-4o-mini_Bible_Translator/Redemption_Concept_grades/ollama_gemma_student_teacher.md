The theological concept of redemption, as it appears throughout the Bible, builds upon this practical understanding. 3. **Cost of Redemption**: The act of redeeming property or a person involved a financial cost, such as buying back land or freeing a relative. 4. **Covenantal Context**: Redemption in ancient Israel was tied to the covenant relationship between God and His people, while Christian redemption is understood within the greater narrative of the New Covenant established by Jesus.
