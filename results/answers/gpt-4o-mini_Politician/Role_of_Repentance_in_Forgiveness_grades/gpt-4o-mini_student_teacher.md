**Forgiveness**, on the other hand, is the act of releasing someone from the consequences of their sins or wrongdoings, and it often involves a compassionate response to those who have wronged us. 3. **Mutual responsibility**: In Christian teachings, believers are also encouraged to forgive others as God forgives them (Colossians 3:13). This aspect highlights the communal nature of these concepts, emphasizing grace and compassion in our relationships.
