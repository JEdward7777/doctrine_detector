1. **Divine Presence**: Often, "fear not" is accompanied by the reassurance of God's presence. For example, in Isaiah 41:10, God says, "Do not fear, for I am with you." This emphasizes that believers are never alone; God is with them in all circumstances, providing comfort and strength.

This verse encourages a reliance on God's faithfulness in all circumstances.
