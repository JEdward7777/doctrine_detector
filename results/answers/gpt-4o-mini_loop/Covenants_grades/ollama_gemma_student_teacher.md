God’s promise to be a God to Israel and for Israel to be His people (Exodus 19:5-6) reflects His desire for holiness and obedience in their relationship. 5. **Davidic Covenant**: God’s promise to David that his throne would be established forever (2 Samuel 7:16) foreshadows the eternal kingship of Christ. This reflects God’s commitment to ensuring that His plan for salvation and reconciliation continues through the lineage of David, culminating in the Messiah.

6. **The New Covenant**: In the New Testament, Jesus establishes a new covenant through His blood (Luke 22:20). This covenant fulfills the earlier promises and reveals God’s ultimate commitment to reconcile humanity through Christ’s sacrifice.
