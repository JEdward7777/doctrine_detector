The term "Christian" is derived from the Greek word "Christianos," meaning "follower of Christ." Biblically, a Christian is one who has accepted Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior and is committed to following His teachings and example.

Several key aspects define a Christian in the Bible:

1. **Faith in Jesus Christ**: A foundational belief is that Jesus is the Son of God and the Savior of humanity. This is underscored in John 3:16, which states, "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

2. **Grace and Salvation**: Christians believe that salvation comes through God's grace alone, not by works. This principle is emphasized in Ephesians 2:8-9, which declares, "For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works, so that no one can boast." This understanding is crucial as it highlights the unmerited favor of God in the redemption process.

3. **Repentance from Sin**: True faith requires repentance—a turning away from sin and a commitment to live in accordance with God’s will. In Acts 2:38, Peter urges the people to "repent and be baptized, every one of you, in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins."

4. **Living According to Christ's Teachings**: Christians are called to embody the teachings of Jesus, living out principles of love, service, and obedience found in the New Testament. For instance, in John 14:15, Jesus says, "If you love me, keep my commands."

5. **Involvement in the Community of Believers**: Being part of a community known as the Church is essential for Christians. The Church is called to fellowship, worship, and support one another. Hebrews 10:24-25 encourages believers to meet together and spur one another on toward love and good deeds.

6. **Transformation of Life**: A Christian life is characterized by transformation, evidenced by the fruit of the Spirit (Galatians 5:22-23), which includes love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

In summary, a Christian is someone who believes in Christ, experiences God's grace for salvation, repents of sin, follows Jesus' teachings, participates in the church community, and lives a transformed life through the Holy Spirit.
