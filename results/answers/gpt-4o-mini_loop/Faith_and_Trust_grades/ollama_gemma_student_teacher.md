1. **Definition**: Faith in a Christian context refers to a belief in God, His promises, and the teachings of Jesus Christ. It encompasses a personal commitment to God, characterized by reliance on Him for salvation and guidance. Trust, on the other hand, is the confidence one has in God's character and faithfulness. It is an active response to faith, reflecting a sense of assurance and security in God.

2. **Response to Promises**: Faith is often viewed as the assurance of things hoped for (Hebrews 11:1), entailing belief in God's promises.
