4. **Contextual Clarity**: Different names of God in the Hebrew Bible highlight various aspects of His character. Translating "YHWH" as "LORD" helps distinguish it from other names, such as "Elohim" (rendered as "God"), aiding readers in navigating the complexities of the text while preserving the unique attributes associated with each name.
