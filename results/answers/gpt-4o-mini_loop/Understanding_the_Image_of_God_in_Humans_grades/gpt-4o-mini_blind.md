4. **Relational Capacity**: Humans are designed for relationships, both with God and with one another. This relational aspect reflects the triune nature of God (Father, Son, Holy Spirit) (Genesis 2:18).

6. **Free Will**: Humans have the ability to choose, reflecting God's freedom. This includes the capacity for love, which must be given voluntarily (Deuteronomy 30:19).

7. **Spirituality**: Humans possess a spiritual dimension that allows them to seek God, worship Him, and have a relationship with the divine, reflecting God's own spirituality (John 4:24).

8. **Value and Dignity**: Being created in God's image grants intrinsic value and dignity to human life, which is emphasized throughout Scripture (Genesis 9:6; Psalm 139:13-14).
