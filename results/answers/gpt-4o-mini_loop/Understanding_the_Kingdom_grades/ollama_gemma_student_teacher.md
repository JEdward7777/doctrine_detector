2. **Old Testament Foundations**: The concept of the kingdom has its foundations in the Old Testament, where God's reign was often associated with the establishment of His covenant with Israel, His chosen people. 5. **Inclusivity**: The New Testament illustrates that the kingdom of God is inclusive, reaching out not just to Jews but to Gentiles as well (Acts 10, Galatians 3:28). 6. **Transformational Power**: The kingdom of God signifies transformation—both personal transformation through repentance and faith (Mark 1:15) and societal transformation as God’s reign actively works against injustice, oppression, and sin (Luke 4:18-19).
