2. **Distinct Persons**: Each of the three persons—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—is fully and completely God, sharing the same divine essence. Yet, they remain distinct in their personhood; the Father is not the Son, and the Son is not the Holy Spirit. All three coexist in unity as one God.

5. **Mystery of the Trinity**: Ultimately, the Trinity remains a profound mystery that surpasses human comprehension. While it is a complex theological concept, Christians embrace it through faith based on the revelation found in Scripture and the historical teachings of the Church.
