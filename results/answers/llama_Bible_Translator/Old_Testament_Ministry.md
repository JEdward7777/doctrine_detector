4. **Bread and wine**: Priests would place bread and wine on the table in front of the temple, symbolizing God's provision and care for His people (Exodus 25:30; Leviticus 24:5-9).

6. **Ritual purity**: Priests were responsible for maintaining their own ritual purity, ensuring that they did not contract impurity through contact with unclean things or people (Leviticus 22:4-7). This underscored the importance of spiritual cleanliness in approaching God.

These practices and rituals were essential to the priests' ministry in the temple, as they provided a means for the people to connect with God and for God to communicate His will.
