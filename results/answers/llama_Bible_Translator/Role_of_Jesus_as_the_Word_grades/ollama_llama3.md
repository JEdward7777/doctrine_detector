Lastly, this title reminds us that Jesus is the ultimate source of all truth, guidance, and inspiration. As the Word of God, He is the one who gives life to all who believe in Him (John 20:31), leading them into a deeper understanding of God's ways and will.

In conclusion, the title "the Word of God" ascribed to Jesus underscores His divinity, role as mediator, authority, trustworthiness, and ultimate significance in Christianity.
