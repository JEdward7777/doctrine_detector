This passage illustrates two key aspects of Christian exaltation:

1. Humility precedes exaltation: Jesus' humility led to His exaltation. This teaches us that we must prioritize humility and submission to God's will in order to experience exaltation.

This passage encourages us to focus on serving others and glorifying God, rather than seeking our own exaltation.

In summary, the passages in 1 Peter 5:5-7 and Philippians 2:9-11 illustrate that Christian exaltation is rooted in humility and obedience to God's will. It's not a means to gain power or recognition, but a natural consequence of living a life submitted to Christ.
