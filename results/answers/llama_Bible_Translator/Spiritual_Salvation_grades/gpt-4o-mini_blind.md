5. Forgiveness of sins: Salvation involves the forgiveness of all past, present, and future sins through the blood of Christ (Colossians 1:14; Ephesians 1:7). This forgiveness brings peace and relief to those who trust in Jesus.
6. Assurance of eternity: Being "saved" provides believers with the assurance that they will spend eternity with God, regardless of what happens in this life (John 5:24).
