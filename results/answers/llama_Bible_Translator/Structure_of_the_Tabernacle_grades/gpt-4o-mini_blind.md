* The Menorah (a seven-branched lampstand) with its oil and wicks
* The Table of Shewbread (also known as the Bread table)
* The Altar of Incense

The Holy Place represented God's presence among his people, where they could worship and offer sacrifices.

* The Ark of the Covenant (also known as the Chest)
	+ Inside the Ark: the tablets of the Ten Commandments, Aaron's rod that budded, and a pot of manna
