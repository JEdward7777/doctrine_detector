2. Syncretism: This blends different religious practices and beliefs together, often creating a hybrid system that compromises biblical truth.
3. Humanism: This elevates human achievements, abilities, or personalities to an unjustified level of importance, effectively replacing God as the ultimate source of value and guidance.

In contrast, worshiping the one true God involves:

1. Monotheism: Recognizing God as the sole Creator, Sustainer, and Redeemer of all things.
2. Biblical authority: Submitting to Scripture as our ultimate source of truth and guidance.
3. Christ-centeredness: Focusing on Jesus Christ, the Son of God, as the central figure of redemption and worship.
