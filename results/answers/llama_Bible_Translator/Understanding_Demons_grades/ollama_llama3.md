4. Illness or affliction: The casting out of unclean spirits is sometimes linked to physical healing or deliverance from various forms of illness or affliction (Mark 5:1-20).

In summary, "unclean spirit" in Christian teachings signifies a type of demon that embodies opposition to God's will, moral corruption, spiritual darkness, and affliction.
