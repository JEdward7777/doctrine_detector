3. Love and Compassion: Humans are capable of experiencing and expressing love, empathy, and kindness towards others, mirroring Allah's boundless love for humanity.
4. Creativity: Humans possess creativity, imagination, and the ability to create art, music, and literature, reflecting God's own creative power in shaping the universe.

These qualities, found in humans, demonstrate that they were created in the image of God (Isha' Allah). As Muslims, we believe that humans have a unique role in this world, as the vicegerents of Allah on Earth. We are entrusted to manage and govern His creation, while also striving to reflect His attributes and characteristics.

May these reflections bring us closer to our Lord, and may we strive to embody these noble qualities in our daily lives.
