1. Timelessness: Eternity transcends the limitations of human time, which is bound by past, present, and future. In eternity, all moments coexist simultaneously.
2. Timelessness in relation to God: Since God is eternal, His existence predates the creation of the universe and will continue long after it ceases to exist. This means that for God, there is no "before" or "after"; He simply exists eternally.

1. Eternal life: Believers anticipate an eternal life after physical death, where they will live in harmony with God and enjoy a restored creation.
2. Heaven: The destination for righteous souls, heaven is often described as a realm where the redeemed will dwell eternally with God.
3. Hell: In contrast, those who reject God's offer of salvation are said to suffer eternal separation from Him, often referred to as hell or the lake of fire.
