Earthly authorities, on the other hand, are those who have been given authority by God to govern and protect people in the physical world. This includes government leaders, law enforcement officers, and other servants of Christ who have been entrusted with specific roles and responsibilities.

In both cases, the ultimate authority is Jesus Christ, who is the ultimate leader and servant of all. Spiritual authorities are called to serve and guide people in their spiritual journey, while earthly authorities are called to serve and protect people in their physical needs.
