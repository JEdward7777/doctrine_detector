4. The Parable of the Unforgiving Servant (Matthew 18:21-35): This parable teaches about forgiveness, which is closely related to mercy. However, the servant went out and found a fellow servant who owed him a small amount, and demanded immediate payment. When the fellow servant could not pay, the first servant had him thrown in prison. Hearing about this, the master was angry and handed the first servant over to be tortured until he paid back everything he owed. In this story, God is depicted as the compassionate master who forgives those who repent and ask for mercy.
