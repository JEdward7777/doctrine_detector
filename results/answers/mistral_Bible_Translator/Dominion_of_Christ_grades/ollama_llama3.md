This passage describes Jesus as the rightful heir to an inheritance that includes all the nations, which He will rule over with authority.

4. In Matthew 28:18-20, Jesus gives the Great Commission to His disciples, saying in part, "All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me." This passage emphasizes Jesus' all-encompassing dominion, not only spiritually but also over every realm of human activity.

In summary, according to the Bible, Jesus Christ exercises a spiritual dominion as the Savior and King of Kings, ruling over all things and reigning until He has put all His enemies under His feet.
