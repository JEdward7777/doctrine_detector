8. Gifts for Service: In Ephesians 4:7-13, Paul talks about the gifts given by the Holy Spirit for the building up of the church. These gifts include apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers.

9. Intercession: The Holy Spirit intercedes for believers in their prayers, according to Romans 8:26-27. He helps us when we don't know what to pray or how to pray effectively.
