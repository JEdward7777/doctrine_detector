7. Seeking spiritual growth: Actively studying the Bible, attending church, and participating in small groups or Bible studies are ways to grow spiritually and deepen one's relationship with God, thereby glorifying Him. (Colossians 2:6-7)

8. Being an example of Christ: Believers can glorify God by emulating the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, striving to reflect His character in their thoughts, words, and actions. (1 Peter 2:21)
