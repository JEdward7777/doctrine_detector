The relationship between a person's soul and their relationship with God is deeply interconnected. Here are some key ways this is expressed in scripture:

2. Communion with God: Through faith in Jesus Christ, our souls can commune with God. This is made possible by the Holy Spirit who dwells within believers (Romans 8:9).

3. Salvation: The soul is saved through faith in Jesus Christ (Ephesians 2:8-9). This salvation grants eternal life and a restored relationship with God.

4. Spiritual Growth: The soul is to be nourished spiritually, as demonstrated by Jesus' command to love God with all our heart, soul, mind, and strength (Mark 12:30).

5. Judgment: Ultimately, the state of one's soul will determine their destiny in the afterlife (Revelation 20:12-13). Believers will spend eternity with God, while unbelievers will be separated from Him.

6. Resurrection: Both body and soul will be resurrected at the end of time (John 5:28-29), with believers receiving glorified bodies and dwelling in God's presence forever.
