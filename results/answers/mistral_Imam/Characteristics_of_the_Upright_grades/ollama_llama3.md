2. **Justice**: Upright people strive for fairness and equality in their dealings with others. They seek to treat everyone fairly and justly, regardless of status or circumstance.

3. **Humility**: Humility is a crucial quality for an upright person. They recognize their limitations and acknowledge their dependence on God. This often leads them to be compassionate and empathetic towards others.

5. **Wisdom and Integrity**: Upright individuals make decisions based on wisdom and integrity. They consider the consequences of their actions and strive to act in a way that is morally right, even when it is difficult.

6. **Righteousness**: Righteousness involves living in accordance with God's law and seeking to do what is good and just. An upright person does not merely avoid wrongdoing but actively pursues righteousness.

7. **Patience and Self-Control**: Upright people exhibit patience and self-control, especially when faced with adversity or temptation. They resist the urge to act impulsively or react harshly.

8. **Honesty**: Honesty is a fundamental quality of an upright person. They are truthful in their words and actions and strive to be transparent and trustworthy.
