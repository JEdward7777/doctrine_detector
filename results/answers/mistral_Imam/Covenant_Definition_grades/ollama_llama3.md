The concept of covenant appears frequently in both the Old Testament (Tanakh) and the New Testament, with well-known examples being the Covenant between God and Abraham (Genesis 15), the Mosaic Covenant (Exodus 24), and the New Covenant established by Jesus Christ (Luke 22:20).
