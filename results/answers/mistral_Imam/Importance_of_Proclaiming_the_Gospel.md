In Christianity, the "Good News" (or Gospel) refers to the life, teachings, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ, which are seen as a path to salvation and eternal life with God.

2. Witnessing: Christians are called to be witnesses of their faith, spreading the message of love, forgiveness, and salvation that they believe in (Matthew 28:19-20).

3. Obedience to God: In the Christian Bible, there are many instances where God commands his followers to spread His message. For example, Matthew 24:14 states, "And this gospel of the kingdom will be preached in the whole world as a testimony to all nations, and then the end will come."
