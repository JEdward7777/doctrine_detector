1. Daily Rituals (Exodus 29; Leviticus 6):

2. Annual Rituals (Leviticus 16, 23):

3. Special Rituals:
   - Consecrating priests and leaders (Exodus 40:13; Leviticus 8).
   - Dedicating the Tabernacle or Temple (Numbers 7; Ezekiel 43-46).
