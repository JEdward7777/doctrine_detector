However, it's essential to note that the concept of predestination in Christianity significantly differs from the Islamic perspective. In Islam, God (Allah) is merciful and just, and human actions are not predestined but are a consequence of free will. The Quran emphasizes the importance of good deeds and accountability for one's actions on the Day of Judgment.
