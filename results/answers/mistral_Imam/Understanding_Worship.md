 In Islam, the concept of worship (Ibada) encompasses a wide range of actions that bring us closer to Allah (God). It is essentially submitting ourselves willingly to the will of Allah in every aspect of our lives. This submission is reflected in five fundamental practices (Arkani), known as the Five Pillars of Islam, which are the Declaration of Faith (Shahada), Prayer (Salah), Zakat (charity), Fasting during Ramadan (Sawm), and Pilgrimage to Makkah (Hajj) once in a lifetime for those who are able.

However, worship goes beyond these five practices.
