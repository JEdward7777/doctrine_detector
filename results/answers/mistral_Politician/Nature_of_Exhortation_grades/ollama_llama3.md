6. Wisdom and discernment: Proverbs 16:16 reminds us that "the road of the wise leads to life, while the ways of fools leads to death." As we exhort others, let's strive for wisdom in our words, seeking guidance from God and being sensitive to the unique circumstances and needs of those around us.

In all things, let our motivation be love (1 Corinthians 13:1-7), striving to build up, encourage, and edify one another in Christ Jesus (Ephesians 4:29).
