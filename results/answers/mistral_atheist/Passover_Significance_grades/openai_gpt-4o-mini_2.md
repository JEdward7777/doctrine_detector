However, it's crucial to remember that while Christians observe this connection, the actual Passover Seder practiced by Jews today differs significantly from the Last Supper narratives. The Christian practice is a commemoration of Jesus' last meal and his sacrificial death rather than a reenactment of the original Passover in Egypt.
