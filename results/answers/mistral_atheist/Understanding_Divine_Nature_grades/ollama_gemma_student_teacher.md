1. Omnipotence (all-powerful): The ability to do anything that does not involve a logical contradiction.
2. Omniscience (all-knowing): The complete knowledge of all things past, present, and future.
3. Omniscience (omnipresent): The presence everywhere at the same time.
4. Moral perfection: The embodiment of goodness, righteousness, and justice.
5. Eternity: Existence without beginning or end.
6. Transcendence: Being above the material world and its limitations.
7. Unchanging nature: God is immutable or unchangeable in essence, unlike human beings who are subject to change over time.
8. Creator of the universe: God is the cause or source of the existence of the universe and everything within it.
9. Self-existence: God does not depend on anything else for his/her/its own existence.
10. Holy: Having moral purity, sacredness, and being set apart from the profane world.
11. Righteous judge: God as a just ruler who punishes evil and rewards good.
12. Infinite love: The capacity to love infinitely and unconditionally.
13. Sovereignty: Supreme authority, dominion, or rule over all things.
