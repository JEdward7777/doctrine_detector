The Bible often advises against this kind of fear, encouraging believers to trust God instead (Proverbs 29:25).

In contrast, everyday fear commonly refers to feelings of anxiety, apprehension, or worry about specific situations or events in our personal lives. It is not always tied to reverence for a higher power and may not necessarily guide one's actions towards wisdom or away from compromise.
