# Question God's Name
## Question
What is the significance of God's name 'Yahweh' as revealed in the Bible?

## Answer from notes
'Yahweh' means 'he is' or 'I am' and signifies God's eternal existence and unchanging nature. It emphasizes His role as the one true God.
